# Kevin-Cookie-Company-Sales
I recently explored a sales dataset for Kevin Cookie Company (2019–20) to analyze what drives their revenue and unit sales.

Key Insights from the analysis:
📊 Chocolate Chip Dominance: Chocolate Chip cookies are the clear crowd favorite, generating 36% of total revenue across all store outlets combined.
🏆 Top 10 Brand Leaders: Rustic Batch Baking Co. claimed the #1 spot among the top 10 brands by units sold.
🏪 Top Performing Outlet: Evergreen Trading is the ultimate hotspot, selling 41% of those Chocolate Chip cookies.
📉 The Bottom Performers: Niche flavors like Fortune (2%) and Sugar Cookies (6%) make up less than 10% of total revenue combined.

🛠️ Skills & Tools Applied:

Data Analysis & Aggregation
Pivot Tables
Interactive Chart Design & Slicers

💡 The Recommendation: Shift warehouse space and resources away from these niche flavors to maximize profits on top sellers.
